1/*!

3 * \brief MUSLE (Modified Universal Soil Loss Equation, Williams, 1995) method to
4 * calculate sediment yield of each cell.

16 * -# Updates USLE_C factor during the growth cycle of the plant.
17 * -# Change the calculation of LS factor, and add USLE_L and USLE_S as outputs.
18 * - 4. 2021-10-29 - ss,lj -
19 * - 5. 2022-08-22 - lj - Change float to FLTPT.
20 *
21 * \author Liangjun Zhu, Zhiqiang Yu
22 */
